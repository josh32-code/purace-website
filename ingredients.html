Our Ingredients

Nature's finest botanicals, cold-pressed and carefully sourced for maximum potency.

Ingredient Philosophy

Every ingredient serves a purpose

We believe in radical transparency. Each botanical, oil, and extract in our formulations is chosen for its proven benefits and sustainable sourcing. No fillers, no synthetic fragrances—just pure, effective ingredients that work in harmony with your skin.

Key Botanicals

Hinoki Wood

Japan

A rare Japanese cypress prized for centuries in traditional wellness practices. Calms, purifies, and supports skin clarity with its gentle, wood-derived compounds.

✓ Calming ✓ Purifying

Camellia Oil

Asia

Cold-pressed from tea plant seeds, this silky oil has been a beauty secret for generations. Rich in oleic acid and vitamins, it deeply nourishes without heaviness.

✓ Nourishing ✓ Anti-aging

Patchouli

Indonesia

An earthy botanical known for its ability to balance oil production and reduce the appearance of blemishes. Grounds the senses while refining skin texture.

✓ Balancing ✓ Clarifying

Jojoba Oil

USA

Technically a liquid wax ester that closely mimics skin's natural sebum. Absorbs quickly, balances moisture, and supports a healthy skin barrier.

✓ Hydrating ✓ Barrier Support

Meadowfoam Seed Oil

Pacific Northwest, USA

One of the most stable plant oils with exceptional shelf life. Creates a protective moisture seal while delivering omega-9 fatty acids for soft, supple skin.

✓ Moisturizing ✓ Protective

Vitamin E (Tocopherol)

Plant-derived

A powerful antioxidant that protects against environmental stressors and supports skin renewal. Preserves the integrity of our precious botanical oils.

✓ Antioxidant ✓ Protective

Cedarwood

USA

Steam-distilled from cedar trees, this grounding oil tones and refines the appearance of skin texture while imparting a warm, woody aroma.

✓ Toning ✓ Refining

Blue Cypress

Australia

A unique Australian botanical with a distinctive blue hue from natural azulene. Soothes and tones skin while supporting a balanced, radiant complexion.

✓ Soothing ✓ Toning

What You Won't Find

We're committed to clean formulations. Here's what we always leave out:

Parabens

Synthetic preservatives that can disrupt hormonal balance.

Sulfates

Harsh detergents that strip skin of natural oils.

Synthetic Fragrance

Chemical compounds that can irritate sensitive skin.

Phthalates

Plasticizers linked to environmental and health concerns.

Mineral Oil

Petroleum-derived occlusive that can clog pores.

Artificial Colors

Unnecessary dyes with no skincare benefit.
